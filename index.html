About Us

Established in 1902, Seetha Hotel is one of Colombo's most historic and elegant hotels. For over a century, we have welcomed guests from around the world with warm hospitality, comfort, and timeless charm. Located in the heart of the city, our hotel blends classic architecture with modern comforts. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Seetha Hotel offers a peaceful and stylish stay in the middle of Colombo's vibrant atmosphere. With beautifully designed rooms, delicious dining, and friendly service, we aim to make every guest feel at home. Experience tradition. Stay in comfort. Welcome to Seetha Hotel.
